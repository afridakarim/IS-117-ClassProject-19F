AFRICA

Cape Town, South Africa

Visit the beautiful port city of CapeTown! Located in southwest coast of South Africa, this city is buzzing with life and activities. You can visit its beaches at Blue Flag Beaches. Or if you're into museums, stop by Robben Island Museum.

Mountain Kilimanjaro, Tanzania

If you're feeling very adventerous, trek onto Mountain Kilimanjaro! It has three volcanic cones: Mawenzi, Shira and Kibo. It happens to be the tallest mountain in Africa and the highest free-standing mountain in the world.

Kruger National Park, South Africa

Who needs a zoo when you can take the family to the beautiful landscape of Kruger National Park. Home to animals such as lions, leopards, rhinos, elephants and buffalos as well as birds like vultures, eagles and storks. Enjoy seeing the animals in their natural habitat!
