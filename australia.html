Let's not forget the beautiful country of New Zealand! Auckland is a major city in New Zealand, it also happens to be located between to large harbours. For the history lovers, The Auckland War Memorial Museum Tāmaki Paenga Hira is the place to go! It is one of New Zealand's most important museums and war memorials. The musuem's focus is moslty on New Zealand history, natural history, and military history
