Eiffel Tower, France

The Eiffel Tower can be described as a global cultural icon. If you stick around at night time, you can surely catch the Eiffel Tower lit with many lights! And if you wish to go to the top, well let's hope you brought your sneakerss, the tower has about 300 steps to the top! Maybe the elevator isn't a bad idea

Buckingham Palace, England

For a taste of British royalty, be sure to visit the historical palace. Who knows, you might get a chance to see a member of the royal family!

The Colosseum, Italy

The Colosseum also known as the Flavian Amphitheatre, is an oval amphitheatre in the center of Rome, Italy. This amphitheatre was used for gladiator contests and animal hunts, mock sea battles, re-enactments of famous battles, executions and dramas. Fun fact, the Colosseum held about 50,000 people!
