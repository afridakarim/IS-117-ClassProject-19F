SOUTH AMERICA

Angel Falls, Venezuela

If you're not afraid of heights, definitely visit this force of nature! Standing at 979 metres and with a plunge of 807 m, Angel Falls is the world's highest uninterrupted waterfall. Wouldn't want to get too close to the edge of that one!

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

If you're one to party, this place is for you! The city is known for its shanty towns calledfavelas and its lively and vibrant Carnaval festival, that features parade floats, flamboyant costumes and samba dancers. These festivals are considered the world’s largest types!

Machu Picchu, Peru

The structures of the Machu Picchu are still a mystery of what their uses were. They are considered one of the most famous icon of Inca civilization.
